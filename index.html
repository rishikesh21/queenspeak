Expert Verdict

Why It Works

At 1.4% interest, your borrowing cost (S$1,838/mo interest) is far below market rent (S$5,800–7,000/mo). Rental income almost entirely covers the mortgage. You build S$216K in equity while the asset appreciates. SSD window closes naturally at Jun 2029 — no forced timing.

The Trade-off

Phase 1 runs a S$1,775/month shortfall in cash (after all holding costs). Over 3 years that's S$63,910 you fund from savings. Phase 2 costs S$6,792/month to live. Total additional cash beyond initial S$602,600: ~S$145,413 across 4 years.

Bottom Line

Buy at S$2.1M. Rent until SSD clears (Jun 2029). Move in for 1 year. Sell Jun 2030. Net profit S$160,624 at 6.66%/yr annualised — comfortably above CPF OA (2.5%) and fixed deposits. Clean, structured, and SSD-free.
