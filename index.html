The Plan in Plain English

  • You buy a 3-bedroom unit on the 28th floor of Queens Peak in June 2026. Bank lends you 75%. You put in the rest.
  • You get the keys in September 2026 and immediately find a tenant who pays S$5,800/month in rent.
  • The rent almost covers your monthly mortgage of S$5,360. You top up only S$910/month extra — less than what many pay in car instalments.
  • After 3 years (June 2029), you can move in, but Seller's Stamp Duty has not cleared yet. The 0% SSD date is after the 4-year holding mark.
  • Sell on or after 15 June 2030. That keeps SSD at 0%, lets the 4-year strategy complete, and preserves the projected S$151,045 profit.
